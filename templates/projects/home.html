About Me

I'm a Data Scientist with a Master's degree in Statistical Data Analytics, a Bachelor's in Mathematics, and an Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree. I'm currently based in Tampere, Finland. I specialise in building and deploying machine learning systems end-to-end — from data processing and model training to production monitoring and CI/CD pipelines.

My focus is on the intersection of ML Engineering and MLOps: making models reliable, reproducible, and observable in production. This portfolio is itself a live MLOps project — containerised with Docker, deployed via GitHub Actions, and equipped with a real-time prediction monitoring dashboard.

I'm actively looking for ML Engineer or Data Scientist roles in Finland or remote, where I can apply both my modelling expertise and my growing infrastructure skills.
